# Missing Devices Research

This document lists missing devices identified for the Cine Power Planner database.
All data has been double-verified with manufacturer specifications or trusted retailers.

## Batch 6: Chargers & Wireless (src/data/devices/chargers.js, src/data/devices/wirelessReceivers.js)

### Chargers
*   **Core SWX Mach4 (V-Mount / Gold-Mount)**
    *   Specs: 4-position simultaneous charger, 4A per channel.
    *   Power Draw: ~260W (64W per channel).
    *   Weight: ~2.3 kg (5 lbs).
    *   Dimensions: 31.5 x 22.6 x 8 cm.
*   **Core SWX Fleet Q4A (V-Mount / Gold-Mount)**
    *   Specs: 4-position simultaneous charger, 3A per channel.
    *   Power Draw: ~175W (43W per channel).
    *   Weight: ~2.3 kg (5 lbs).
*   **IDX VL-2000S (V-Mount)**
    *   Specs: 2-channel simultaneous charger, 4A (1 batt) / 3A (2 batts).
    *   Power Draw: 100W DC output, max ~160W input.
    *   Weight: ~1.24 kg (2.74 lbs).
*   **IDX VL-4S (V-Mount)**
    *   Specs: 4-channel simultaneous charger, 3A (1-3 batts) / 2.3A (4 batts).
    *   Power Draw: Max 310VA.
    *   Weight: ~3.1 kg (6.83 lbs).

### Wireless Receivers
*   **Teradek Bolt 6 LT HDMI RX**
    *   Specs: 6GHz wireless video receiver, HDMI only.
    *   Power Draw: 11W.
    *   Weight: ~304g (10.7 oz).
*   **Teradek Bolt 6 LT 750 RX**
    *   Specs: 6GHz wireless video receiver, SDI/HDMI, 750ft range.
    *   Power Draw: 11W.
    *   Weight: ~292g (10.3 oz).
*   **DJI High-Bright Remote Monitor (RX)**
    *   Specs: Integrated receiver and 7" high-bright monitor.
    *   Power Draw: ~12-15W (Monitor + RX).
    *   Weight: ~727g.
*   **DJI Video Receiver (Standard)**
    *   Specs: Standalone O3 Pro video receiver.
    *   Power Draw: 9W.
    *   Weight: ~350g.
