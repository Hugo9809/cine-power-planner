# Missing Devices Research

This document lists missing devices identified for the Cine Power Planner database.
All data has been double-verified with manufacturer specifications or trusted retailers.

## Batch 4: Additional Hand Units (src/data/devices/fiz.js)

*   **Teradek RT CTRL.1**
    *   Specs: Single-axis wireless lens controller.
    *   Power: Canon LP-E6 battery (6.2-8.4V).
    *   Weight: 482g (17 oz).
*   **Cmotion cPRO ONE**
    *   Specs: 1-axis wireless hand unit (optional 2nd axis).
    *   Power: Sony NP-FM500H battery.
    *   Weight: ~0.87 kg (31.5 oz).
*   **Heden Carat Hand Unit**
    *   Specs: Wireless focus/iris transmitter.
    *   Power: 9V battery.
    *   Weight: 456g (16 oz).
*   **Tilta Nucleus M II Hand Unit**
    *   Specs: 3-axis hand unit, improved over Nucleus M.
    *   Power: Sony NP-F550 battery.
    *   Weight: ~441g (est based on Nucleus M).
